For years, the empire had been divided into seven provinces because the old king had split the throne among his daughters before the first mana famine. The famine changed everything about the academy, the noble houses, the currency, the shape of military service, the southern road, and the way commoners thought about rank. Before Kellan entered the trial hall, he remembered how his grandfather had described the long march and how the guilds had betrayed the crown in the rain.

Kellan opened the door.
